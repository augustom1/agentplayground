# Target Customers & Ideal Client Profiles

## Primary Markets

### 1. Argentine Freelancers & Developers
- Works with small businesses, builds web apps or handles IT
- Wants to offer "AI automation" as a service to their own clients
- Buys **Stack Completo** to deploy on their own server, then resells access to clients
- Budget-conscious; prefers to invest in tools that grow with them
- Finds us: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Argentine dev communities (Telegram groups, Discord)
- **Pain point:** Clients ask for AI features but they don't have a ready platform to deliver

### 2. Small Digital Agencies (5–20 people)
- Digital marketing, e-commerce, or SaaS agencies
- Want internal AI tools for content generation, data analysis, client reporting
- Has a technical person who can manage it, but doesn't want to build from scratch
- Buys **Premium** for the support and onboarding
- Finds us: referrals, LinkedIn, Google
- **Pain point:** ChatGPT is not enough — they need an integrated system with memory and workflows

### 3. Solo Founders / Non-Technical Entrepreneurs
- Has a business idea or early startup
- Understands AI matters but doesn't know how to implement it
- Needs hand-holding → **Premium tier**
- Finds us: LinkedIn, Instagram, word of mouth
- **Pain point:** Can't hire a dev team yet; needs AI leverage to punch above their weight

### 4. Technical CTOs / Dev Leads
- Wants full control, hates SaaS lock-in
- Evaluates the GitHub repo first
- Probably **Básico or Stack Completo**
- Finds us: GitHub, Hacker News, dev Twitter
- **Pain point:** Existing AI platforms are black boxes; wants to own the stack

## Client Qualification Questions

Before proposing, ask:
1. Do you already have a VPS, or do you need help getting one? (Hetzner recommended)
2. What's your primary use case? (content, support, research, internal ops?)
3. Do you have technical staff, or will this be fully managed?
4. What's your monthly budget for tools/services?
5. What AI tools are you using now? (OpenAI, ChatGPT, Zapier?)

## Red Flags (Don't Pursue)

- Clients who want a guarantee of ROI before buying
- Clients who can't meet minimum server requirements (< 4GB RAM)
- Clients expecting real-time consumer-grade support (we're async)
