# AgentPlayground — Vision & Strategic Direction

## The Big Picture

We're building the **operating system for personal and team AI work** — but self-hosted, which means the user owns everything: their agents, their memory, their data.

The analogy: like Obsidian is to note-taking (ownable, local-first, extensible), AgentPlayground is to AI-assisted work.

## The Flywheel

1. **User talks to the Keeper** → task gets dispatched to specialized agent teams
2. **Agents do the work** → results saved to the 2nd Brain (vault)
3. **Brain accumulates knowledge** → future agent responses are richer and more personalized
4. **Platform learns to route cheap tasks to local LLMs** → costs drop, speed increases
5. **User talks more** → flywheel spins faster
