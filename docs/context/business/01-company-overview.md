## What This Is

AgentPlayground is a self-hosted AI operations platform built and run by Augusto Meyer. The core idea: replace the entire business back-office (content, client comms, billing, reporting, research, admin) with AI agent teams that work around the clock and report back through a single coordinator interface.

There are two parallel purposes:

**Personal OS** — Augusto uses it himself to manage his freelance business, personal goals (CV, education, finance, fitness), and all dev work. The platform IS the business operations tool.

**SaaS Product** — Eventually sold to other operators (agencies, freelancers, consultants in Latin America) who want to run AI-managed digital services businesses. Each operator gets their own private workspace.

## Mission

Give every freelancer and small agency in Latin America access to an AI team that does the work they can't afford to hire humans for: content, outreach, reporting, client onboarding, scheduling, billing follow-up, research.

---

## What Makes This Different

1. **Coordinator-first**: One AI interface delegates everything. User never has to think about which agent does what.
2. **Self-improving**: The Brain accumulates context. Agents get better as they learn patterns from past work.
3. **Local-first AI**: Routine tasks run on local Ollama models (zero cost). Claude handles only complex reasoning.
4. **Operator-run**: You host it on your own VPS. No data goes to SaaS intermediaries. Clients trust it more.
