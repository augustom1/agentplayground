# Services & Pricing — AgentPlayground

> Used by Business & Growth team when drafting proposals, responding to client inquiries, or refining pricing strategy.

---

## What We Sell

Three subscription tiers. All include coordinator access. Price is per operator workspace (not per end user).

### Tier 1 — Personal ($19/mo)
**Who it's for:** Individuals who want a personal AI operations center.

Includes:
- Coordinator chat (unlimited)
- 3 personal agent teams (CV Advisory, Education, Financial Planner)
- Brain (knowledge base, up to 1,000 documents)
- Telegram integration (personal DMs only)
- Local LLM routing (Ollama on shared VPS)

### Tier 2 — Business ($49/mo)
**Who it's for:** Freelancers and consultants who serve clients.

Includes everything in Personal, plus:
- Business & Growth team (proposals, client strategy, growth analysis)
- Job Search team
- 5 client project slots (via Projects dashboard)
- Weekly automated business reports to Brain
- Billing monitor agent (crypto payment detection)
- Admin panel access (analytics, API usage)

### Tier 3 — Agency ($149/mo)
**Who it's for:** Agencies delivering digital services at scale.

Includes everything in Business, plus:
- Unlimited client project slots
- White-label: custom subdomain + branding
- Multi-client Brain namespacing
- Dedicated VPS sizing recommendation
- Dev Core + DevOps teams included
- Priority support (coordinator responds with 30-tool depth)
- Client-facing chatbot deployment per client

## Pricing Philosophy

- USD pricing for international clients; ARS pricing for Argentine local clients
- Monotributo invoicing for local clients; Wyoming LLC for international (when opened)
- Never discount below 70% of list price without business team approval
- First client may get 50% off for 3 months as case study — coordinator can authorize this
- Always offer annual billing at 20% discount (2 months free)

---

## Value Proposition (Pitch Points)

1. **Cost**: A junior VA costs $500–1,500/mo. An AI team doing the same work costs $19–149/mo.
2. **Speed**: Agents work 24/7. A report that takes a human Friday afternoon takes agents 8 minutes at 3am.
3. **Memory**: The Brain remembers everything. No briefing the same context twice.
4. **Control**: Hosted on your own VPS. No SaaS vendor lock-in. Your data stays yours.
5. **Delegation**: One coordinator interface. No context switching between 10 different tools.

---

## What's NOT Included

- Guaranteed response SLAs (best effort from VPS capacity)
- Human-reviewed deliverables (all AI-generated, human reviews before publishing)
- Compliance advice (accounting summaries are operational, not tax advice)
- API costs above $20/mo (overage billed at cost)
