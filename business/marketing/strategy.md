# Marketing Strategy

## Situation

- **Stage:** Pre-revenue. Zero clients. No reviews yet.
- **Budget:** ~$0 for paid ads (at least initially).
- **Advantage:** Unique product in the Argentine market. No direct local competitors for "install-your-own AI platform as a service."
- **Constraint:** The founder is also the delivery person. Every sale = real work.

---

## Core Message (to refine)

**English:** "We install a full AI operations platform on your VPS. You own it forever."

**Spanish options:**
- "Instalamos tu plataforma de IA en tu servidor. Costo único, tuyo para siempre."
- "Tu stack de IA, en tu servidor, sin suscripciones. Lo armamos nosotros."
- "Automatizá tu empresa con agentes de IA. Sin depender de servicios externos."

**Key anxiety to address:** "I don't understand Docker / Linux / servers."
**Key desire:** "I want AI tools working in my business without becoming a DevOps engineer."

---

## Channels (priority order)

### 1. LinkedIn (highest priority, free)
**Why:** Argentine professionals who make buying decisions are on LinkedIn. B2B service discovery happens here.

**Tactics:**
- Post case studies once we have 1–2 clients ("Set up a full AI stack for a marketing agency in Buenos Aires in 3 hours")
- Post educational content: "¿Qué es n8n y por qué toda agencia debería tenerlo?"
- Post "before/after" — what took 4 hours manually now takes 0 seconds with automation
- Comment on posts about AI tools, automation, Zapier, ChatGPT (offer the self-hosted alternative)
- Connect with: agency owners, startup CTOs, freelancers offering digital marketing

**Frequency:** 3–4 posts/week while building audience

---

### 2. Twitter/X
**Why:** Dev community, tech-forward founders, Latin American tech Twitter is active.

**Tactics:**
- Document the build in public: "Building an AI ops platform in Argentina — thread 🧵"
- Share the open-source repo
- Reply to threads about AI tools with the self-hosted angle
- Target: devs who complain about SaaS pricing ("tired of paying $99/mo for Zapier")

---

### 3. Argentine Freelancer Communities
**Why:** Freelancers are multipliers — they can buy for themselves AND resell to their clients.

**Groups to target:**
- Facebook: "Freelancers Argentina", "Desarrolladores Web Argentina", "Community Managers Argentina"
- Reddit: r/Argentina (tech threads), r/LaArgentinaDeArgentines
- Telegram/Discord: dev communities, n8n communities, no-code communities
- Comunidad MercadoLibre freelancers (if applicable)

**Message:** "Instalá una plataforma de automatización completa en tu VPS y ofrecéla como servicio a tus clientes"

---

### 4. SEO / Content (medium term)
**Why:** Organic traffic compounds over time.

**Target keywords (Spanish):**
- "como instalar n8n en vps"
- "plataforma de agentes ia self hosted"
- "alternativa a zapier autoalojada"
- "instalar ollama servidor propio"
- "automatizacion ia sin pagar suscripcion"

**Content ideas:**
- "Guía: Instalá n8n en tu VPS con Docker y Traefik en 30 minutos"
- "Ollama vs ChatGPT API: cuándo usar cada uno"
- "Por qué mi agencia se mudó de Zapier a n8n (y ahorramos $X/mes)"

---

### 5. Referrals (best ROI, medium term)
- Offer 15% commission to anyone who refers a paying client
- Target: IT consultants, web developers, digital agencies who service SMBs

## Pricing Psychology Notes

- $49 USD is an impulse buy for a professional. Minimize friction.
- $149 is "consideration purchase" — they'll think for a day. The sales page needs good social proof.
- $299 needs a conversation, not just a page.
- Consider offering a "money back if it doesn't work" guarantee (we control the installation so we can guarantee it works).
- ARS prices change with the dollar — keep prices in USD on the site to avoid having to update constantly.
